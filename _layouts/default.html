Jiancheng Dong

Greetings! I am currently a first-year Ph.D. student at the City University of Hong Kong, where I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Xiangyu Zhao.

My research interests primarily lie in LLMs, with a specific focus on long sequence compression and packing.

Previously, I received my bachelor's degree from the School of Artificial Intelligence at Nanjing University. During my undergraduate studies, I worked as a Research Intern at the Responsible and Reliable AI Lab at the University of Illinois Chicago under the supervision of Prof. Lu Cheng and Prof. Wei Jin. Before that, I was also an intern at the NJU NLP Group, advised by Prof. Xinyu Dai and Prof. Zhen Wu.
