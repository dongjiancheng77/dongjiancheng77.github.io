Jiancheng Dong

Greetings! I am a senior student at the School of Artificial Intelligence, Nanjing University. Currently, I am working as a Research Intern at the Responsible and Reliable AI Lab (R2 Lab) at the University of Illinois Chicago under the supervision of Prof. Lu Cheng and Prof. Wei Jin.

Previously, I was an intern at NJU NLP Group, advised by Prof. Xinyu Dai and Prof. Zhen Wu.

My research interests lie in Natural Language Processing, with a focus on advancing the reasoning capabilities and efficiency of large language models (LLMs). My work centers on designing methods to enhance alignment and robustness in LLM reasoning (e.g., mathematical problem-solving and code generation), and optimizing fine-tuning processes to reduce computational costs while maintaining high performance(TFP). By bridging efficient training techniques with reliable reasoning frameworks, I aim to build trustworthy AI systems that balance technical rigor with practical scalability.

Actively seeking PhD opportunities!
