I developed  **DiVA-Former** , a lightweight framework for compressing long structured contexts in LLMs. It uses visual tokens as dynamic queries to distill lengthy serialized table text into compact digest vectors, reducing context length and computational burden while preserving both structural cues and  fine-grained textual information.
